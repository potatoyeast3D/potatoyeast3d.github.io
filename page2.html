Blender Times


Saketh's computer is quite strong but it still takes alot of time to render. This is because of the way rendering works. The camera precalculates the paths that its rays will take and then shoots its rays out and the ray's colors are effected by the materials they touch and the distance from the camera, giving color and depth. This process takes alot of processing power, but gives acurate results. Here is a data table on render times.
